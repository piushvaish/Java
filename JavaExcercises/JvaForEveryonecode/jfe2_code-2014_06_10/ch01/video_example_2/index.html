Developing an Algorithm
Understand the Problem

The task:

Develop an algorithm for dividing household expenses among roommates.

What are the inputs?

  • Incoming bills: Amounts and purpose
  • Example: $82.54 for Electricity
  • Names of roommates
  • Example: Diana, Juliet, Mary, Romeo, and Tom

What are the desired outputs?

  • For each roommate, how much they have to pay.
  • Example: Diana $334.55, Juliet $328.11, Mary $334.55, etc.

How are the outputs determined?

  • Generally, each item is divided equally among all roommates.
  • Romeo and Juliet don't watch cable, so they don't have to pay the cable bill.
  • Romeo pays twice the rent of everyone else because he has a large single room.

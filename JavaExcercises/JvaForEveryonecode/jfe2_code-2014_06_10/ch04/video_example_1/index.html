Evaluating Cell Phone Plans
Understand the Problem

Cell phone costs

  • Phone
  • Monthly fee includes allowance for minutes/messages/megabyte
  • Fees for additional minutes/messages/megabyte
Calculate by Hand

Sample plan

  • Phone: Free
  • Monthly fee $69.95 includes 700 minutes, no messages, 300 megabytes
  • Additional minutes $0.40, messages $0.20, megabytes $0.067
  • My usage: 900 minutes, 60 messages, 200 megabytes
Design an Algorithm 
Prompt for minutes used, messages used, megabytes used.
Name of cheapest plan = ""
Cost of cheapest plan = 0
For each plan
  Prompt for name of plan, phone cost, monthly fee,
    free minutes, messages, megabytes,
    fee for additional minutes, messages, megabytes
  Compute monthly cost
  If name of cheapest plan is "" or cost of cheapest plan > monthly cost
    Name of cheapest plan = name of plan
    Cost of cheapest plan = monthly cost
Display name and cost of cheapest plan

Compute monthly cost
Monthly cost = Cost of phone / 24 + monthly fee
If minutes used > free minutes
  Add (minutes used - free minutes) × additional minute fee to monthly cost
If messages used > free messages
  Add (messages used - free messages) × additional message fee to monthly cost 
If megabyte used > free megabyte
  Add (megabyte used - free megabyte) × additional megabyte fee to monthly cost
